We Deliver the Best Biscuits

Nebico has been serving society with the best biscuits and confectioneries since 1964. Our products are not only delicious but also healthy, enriched with calcium to support the nutritional needs of every Nepalese family. We are committed to quality, innovation, and social responsibility, ensuring that every bite of our biscuits brings joy and health to our customers.

Why Choose Nebico?

Nebico is the pioneer biscuit manufacturer in Nepal, with decades of experience in producing high-quality biscuits and confectioneries. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, ISO 9001:2008 certification, and commitment to social causes set us apart. We prioritize customer satisfaction, ensuring that our products meet the highest standards of taste, health, and quality.

From our classic Glucose Biscuits to our innovative Digestive Biscuits, every product is crafted with care and precision. We continuously invest in research and development to bring new and exciting flavors to our customers while maintaining our commitment to health and nutrition.

Our Guarantee

At Nebico, we guarantee the highest quality in every product we manufacture. Our biscuits are made with the finest ingredients, ensuring a delicious and healthy snack for everyone. We are dedicated to supporting our community through various social initiatives, including sports, cultural programs, and R&D projects. When you choose Nebico, you choose a brand that cares about your health and well-being.

Nebico's commitment to excellence extends beyond our products. We are proud members of the Global Compact, adhering to international standards for sustainability and ethical business practices. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every biscuit we produce meets the expectations of our valued customers. Choose Nebico for a snack that’s not only tasty but also trustworthy and reliable.
