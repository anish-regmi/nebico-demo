Important Notices from Nebico Private Limited

  • 100 Views
  • 35 Comments
  • 24th March 2019

Notice 1: Nebico Private Limited is proud to announce the launch of our new range of healthy and calcium-rich biscuits, designed to support the nutritional needs of every Nepalese family. Stay tuned for more updates!

Notice 2: We are pleased to inform you that Nebico has successfully renewed its ISO 9001:2008 certification, reaffirming our commitment to quality and excellence in biscuit manufacturing.

By Nebico Management

"At Nebico, we are dedicated to serving society with the best biscuits and confectioneries, ensuring taste, health, and quality in every bite."

Notice 3: Nebico is currently operating in two shifts with an annual production capacity of 2,400 MT. We thank our valued customers for their continued support and trust in our products.

Notice 4: We are excited to share that Nebico has been actively supporting social causes, including sports, cultural programs, and R&D initiatives, as part of our commitment to the community.
