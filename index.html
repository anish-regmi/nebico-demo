About Us

Nepal’s Pioneer Biscuit Manufacturer

Nebico Private Limited is the first and leading biscuit manufacturer in Nepal. Established in 1964, we have been producing high-quality biscuits and confectioneries that have become a household name.

Situated in Balaju Industrial Area, Kathmandu, Nebico has been committed to quality and innovation. With certifications like ISO 9001:2008, we ensure the highest standards in our production process. Our products, including Glucose, Thin Arrowroot, Digestive, and Coconut Crunches, have been enjoyed by generations.
